---
title: How it works
---
- Rows and columns still each hold the digits 1–9.
- The straight 3×3 boxes are replaced by nine irregular **regions**.
- Each region, outlined with thick borders, holds 1–9 once.

---
title: Quick example
---
If an irregular region already contains `1` through `8`, its last empty cell must be `9`. Trace each region's odd shape carefully, since they twist across the usual box lines.
