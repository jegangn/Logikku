---
title: How it works
---
- All the usual rules apply: rows, columns, and boxes each hold 1–9.
- A green **path** links cells that must differ sharply.
- Each step along the path changes by at least 5.

---
title: Quick example
---
A cell on the path needs a neighbour at least 5 away, but every digit is within 4 of `5`, so `5` can never sit on a whisper path. Path cells tend to alternate between low and high digits.
