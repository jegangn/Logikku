---
title: How it works
---
- All the usual rules apply: rows, columns, and boxes each hold 1–9.
- A green **path** links cells that must differ sharply.
- Each step along the path changes by at least 5.

---
title: Quick example
---
If a cell on the path holds `5`, its neighbour on the path cannot be `5` plus or minus 4, leaving no valid digit, so `5` is impossible there. Path cells tend to alternate between low and high digits.
