---
title: How it works
---
- All the usual rules apply: rows, columns, and boxes each hold 1–9.
- Cells that touch side by side cannot hold **consecutive** digits.
- So neighbours may never differ by exactly one.

---
title: Quick example
---
If a cell holds `5`, the cells directly above, below, left, and right cannot be `4` or `6`. This quietly removes options from every neighbour and chains across the grid.
