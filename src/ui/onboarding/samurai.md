---
title: How it works
---
- Five 9×9 grids overlap in a cross, one in the centre and one in each corner.
- Each grid is a full Sudoku: every row, column, and box holds 1–9.
- The **shared corner boxes** belong to two grids at once and must satisfy both.

---
title: Quick example
---
A digit placed in a shared box counts toward both the centre grid and a corner grid. Solving one overlap often unlocks a stretch of the neighbouring grid, so work the joins first.
