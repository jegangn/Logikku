---
title: How it works
---
- All the usual rules apply: rows, columns, and boxes each hold 1–9.
- A **V** between two cells means the pair sums to 5.
- An **X** between two cells means the pair sums to 10.

---
title: Quick example
---
A V joins cells that add to 5, so they could be `1` and `4` or `2` and `3`. An edge with no mark sums to neither 5 nor 10, which also removes pairs.
