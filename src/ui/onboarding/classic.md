---
title: How it works
---
- Fill the grid so every row holds the digits 1–9.
- Every column also holds 1–9, with no repeats.
- Each of the nine 3×3 **boxes** holds 1–9 once as well.

---
title: Quick example
---
If a row already shows eight digits and only `7` is missing, the empty cell in that row must be `7`. Look for rows, columns, or boxes that are nearly complete to find the easy placements first.
