---
title: How it works
---
- All the usual rules apply: rows, columns, and boxes each hold 1–9.
- Each **thermometer** starts at a round bulb and runs along a line.
- Digits must strictly increase from the bulb to the tip.

---
title: Quick example
---
On a four-cell thermometer, the bulb can be at most `6`, since the three cells after it must each be larger. The longer the thermometer, the more its ends are pinned down.
